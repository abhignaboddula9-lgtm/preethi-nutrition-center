Our Philosophy

Preethi Raghavan established the center with a single vision: making nutrition approachable, delightful, and scientific. After working in hospital environments, she realized that healthy habits fail when diets are too restrictive and exercises feel like chores.

By combining custom dietary scheduling with high-energy cardiovascular Zumba routines, the Preethi Nutrition Center offers a unique combination of science-backed clinical nutrition and fun fitness guidance.
